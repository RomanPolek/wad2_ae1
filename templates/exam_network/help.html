We are here to help you.
Below we have answered some frequently asked questions.

Question: I'm a teacher, how do I add a new course?

The good news is this part is really easy. Just log in, click Add Course in the navigation bar at the top (it's underneath the Exam Networl logo!), give your course a name and short description and click "Add".

Question: I'm a teacher, how do I add students to my course?

When you've created your exciting course, if you head to Add Students on the navigation bar, you simply select your course from the dropdown menu (click the little arrow at the right of Select Course). Then you just need to type in the email addresses of the students you wish to add. Be sure to seperate each email address with a comma!.

Question: I'm a teacher, how do I set an exam for my students?

There's a few steps to this part.
First, click Add Exam in the navigation bar.
Next, select the course you're writing the exam for from the dropdown menu, like you did when adding your students.
Now, give your exam a Title by typing it in text box.
Now you need to set the time students will be able to sit the exam. Click the little calendar icon in the Date Available Box, and you'll be able to select a date from the calendar and a time. Do the same for the deadline
Now you click the blue button with the "+" symbol to start adding questions.
Type the question itself in the content box and give one of five potential answers in the answer box. Make sure to click the round button next to the correct answer.
Repeat that for as many questions as you'd like to add.
Finally, if you're happy with what you've got click the blue "Add" button at the bottom and you're done!
If you change your mind at any point you can click the grey "Cancel" button to clear everything out and start again.
